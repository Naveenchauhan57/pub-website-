Frequently Asked Questions

What is the publication process?

Our publication process involves:

  1. Initial submission and screening
  2. Peer review (2-3 weeks)
  3. Author revision (if required)
  4. Final decision and publication

What are the publication charges?

Publication charges vary by journal and article type. Contact our support team for detailed pricing information for your specific submission.

How can I track my submission?

You can track your submission using our article tracking system. Simply enter your submission ID on our tracking page to get real-time updates.
