How to Submit Your Enquiry

  1. Browse through our comprehensive list of journals by category
  2. Click on journals to select/deselect them for your subscription
  3. Fill in your contact information in the enquiry form
  4. Review your selected journals in the form
  5. Add any specific requirements or questions in the message box
  6. Click "Send Enquiry" to submit your request
